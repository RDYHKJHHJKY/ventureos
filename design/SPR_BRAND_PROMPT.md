MASTER BRAND PROMPT (Use this everywhere)

Software Passport Registry — Global Trust Infrastructure
A cold, institutional, compliance‑grade identity system for software.
Visual language inspired by ICANN, IANA, NIST, ISO, and certificate authorities.
Design uses monochrome graphite, registry black, compliance white, and passport blue accents.
UI is sharp, geometric, high‑contrast, and audit‑grade.
Brand tone is formal, deterministic, and evidence‑driven.
Logo is a geometric registry seal with a digital passport motif, lineage nodes, and trust bars.
Everything feels global, authoritative, permanent, and regulatory.
No startup vibes. No marketing fluff. Pure infrastructure.

---

Designer/AI Usage Notes

- Imagery: use metallic textures, engraved stamps, and document motifs. Prefer cold lighting and desaturated palettes with a single vivid passport blue accent.
- Typography: use a neutral, geometric sans (Inter / IBM Plex Sans / Roboto) with high weight contrast for headings. Tight leading, rigid tracking.
- Grid: enforce evenly spaced modular grids; margins should be measured and audit‑grade.
- Iconography: geometric, monoline, precise; avoid soft corners and playful shapes.
- Deliverables: provide SVG logo variations (seal, mark-only, wordmark), a favicon set, and CSS tokens.

Premium / Lux Variant (Option C)

- Summary
  A premium, upscale registry identity that reads like a sovereign institution and a collectible seal.
  Deep black base with gold accents, serif display typography for headings, and precise serif/sans mixtures for body copy.

- Color tokens (Premium)
  - Registry black: #000000
  - Surface: #0A0A0A
  - Border: #141414
  - Compliance white (text): #F8F9FA
  - Premium gold (accent): #C9A86A
  - Accent dim: #A8834D

- Typography
  - Headings: high-contrast serif (Merriweather, Playfair Display)
  - Body: neutral sans (Inter, IBM Plex Sans)
  - Use serif for large display and section headings; use sans for dense tables and UI controls.

- Logo direction adjustments
  - Use gold ring and gold trust-bars on a black field for the seal.
  - Provide a wordmark in gold and a monochrome inverse version.

- Usage guidance
  - Reserve gold accents for key UI highlights: active nav, seal, trust score highlights.
  - Use serif headings sparingly to preserve readability in dense UIs.
  - Maintain high contrast and minimal ornamentation.
